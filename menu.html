Our Heritage

The Heart of Zamboanga del Sur

Molave stands as a beacon of progress and tradition, where natural beauty meets modern development in perfect harmony.

A Legacy of Growth & Unity

Since 1948, Molave has flourished as a first-class municipality, strategically positioned 29 kilometers east of Pagadian City. Named after the majestic Molave tree, our community embodies strength, resilience, and natural beauty.

Our agricultural heritage runs deep, with rice, corn, coconut, and rubber cultivation forming the backbone of our economy. Today, we've evolved into a modern educational hub while preserving our cultural roots through vibrant festivals like the annual Megayon celebration.
